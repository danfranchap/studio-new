Meet Emily

Emily Chapdelaine knew from a very young age that she wanted to be in the beauty industry. She spent a lot of time in her grandmother's salon. Emily attended Michael's School of Hair Design and Aesthetics, a Paul Mitchell Partner School where she received both a cosmetology license and a master barber license. Emily has always had a desire to work with organic, healthy products so she started her career working with Organic Colour Systems. Shortly after starting to work with the Organic Colour line, she became an educator, teaching fellow stylists and colorists the benefits and techniques of organic hair color. Her love of the beauty industry lead her to fashion shoots and bridal makeup. She has partnered with Kriss Cosmetics, a local makeup company, and carries the makeup line in her studio. Studio Em was established in January of 2016. It is a unique, one on one stylist experience in a quiet salon environment.
