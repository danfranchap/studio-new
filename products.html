Color

Studio Em uses some of the most body and earth friendly color on the market. Organic Colour Systems is the first-ever range of permanent colours made from the maximum amount of certified organic ingredients and the minimum amount of chemicals to colour hair as naturally as possible.

Organic Colour Systems' unique approach is gentle but effective and easily colours even resistant grey hair. And because hair responds better to natural ingredients it looks naturally healthier and glossier with radiant, longer-lasting colour.
